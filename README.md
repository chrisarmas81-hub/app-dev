# My Favorite Movies and Series

## 1. Avengers: Endgame

**Avengers: Endgame** (2019) is the culmination of over a decade of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). This epic superhero film features an ensemble cast of characters from the MCU who must come together to undo the catastrophic damage caused by *Thanos*.
 **Genre**: Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi
- **Director**: Anthony and Joe Russo
- **Key Themes**: Heroism, sacrifice, time travel
- **Memorable Quote**: 
> "I love you 3000." – Tony Stark
---
## 2. Death Whisperer (Webtoon)

**Death Whisperer** is a South Korean webtoon series that blends dark fantasy and action with psychological depth. The story follows a man who is given the ability to communicate with the dead.

- **Genre**: Fantasy, Action, Drama
- **Author**: Lee Won-sik
- **Key Themes**: Life, death, redemption, justice
- **Current Status**: Ongoing
  
## 3. Solo Leveling (Webtoon)

**Solo Leveling** (or *I Alone Level Up*) is another popular webtoon that has gained massive acclaim worldwide. It’s set in a world where portals to dangerous dungeons have opened, and people with unique abilities—called Hunters—are tasked with clearing them.

- **Genre**: Fantasy, Action, Adventure
- **Author**: Chugong
- **Status**: Completed (Web Novel) / Ongoing (Webtoon)
- **Key Themes**: Strength, growth, survival, determination

## 4. Central Intelligence

**Central Intelligence** (2016) is a comedy-action film starring *Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson* and *Kevin Hart*. The film follows a mild-mannered accountant who teams up with a CIA agent to prevent a global threat.

- **Genre**: Action, Comedy
- **Director**: Rawson Marshall Thurber
- **Key Themes**: Friendship, teamwork, identity
- **Memorable Quote**: 
  > "I’m just a regular guy. I don’t know what you’re talking about!" – Calvin Joyner
